24U Phone Companion - Compatible PBX as per TeamCall 3.8.19

24U Phone Companion uses ilink TeamCall Lite as a middleware to communicate with your PBX. TeamCall Lite is a limited addition of TeamCall and is available in 2 modification. TeamCall Express only works on Windows with TAPI drivers installed and connects to the PBX via USB or serial port. TeamCall Server is available for macOS, Windows, and Linux and connects to the PBX via TCP/IP.

Up to date online version of this list can be found on 24U Website: http://www.24usoftware.com/support/PBXSupport.

The green rows are the systems added in version 3.0.
